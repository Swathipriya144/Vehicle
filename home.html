ABOUT US

MINI is a very famous brand known for its unique identity like no other. MINI cars have a charm of their own and all MINIs feature an iconic silhouette and body style till date. They are compact in dimension, look cute, are blissful to drive with punchy performance and agile handling, and the list goes on. So what is a MINI? It's basically a small economy car that is known for its speed, extreme drifting, and unique look. ... MINI, which is owned by BMW, is recognized at making some of the most popular small cars. While the Cooper is the most recognized model, there are other cars from MINI to choose from.
